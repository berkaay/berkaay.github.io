Features & Details

Socialize & Play

With the feed you can see what game is hot and what your friends scored. Try to beat them!

Challenge Your Friends

Find out who is a better player by challenging them on a mini-game.

Awards

Earn and collect rare badges that express your gaming style or Unlock an achivement that no-one unlocked.

Real Time

Each Engage game can be played real-time with 2 or more people. See who has better skills.

Groups

Create groups or get into groups. They have their own score list and feed. You may find an opponent that you share an interest.

Location Based

When you can't find someone to play, search people near you to play quick games and get better at local score lists.
